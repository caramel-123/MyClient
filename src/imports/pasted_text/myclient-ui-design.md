Design a complete responsive website UI for MyClient — an AI-powered client simulation platform for students and youth participating in project-based learning.

Product Overview

MyClient helps students learn real-world client communication and project delivery through AI roleplay. Instead of tutorials, students interact with an AI that behaves like a real client and guides them through the entire freelance workflow: requirement gathering, proposal writing, revisions, QA feedback, negotiation, and project delivery.

Core experience:
The AI messages the student first.

Tone:
Modern • Friendly • Professional • Youth-focused • Slightly playful but credible • Educational SaaS

Target users:

* IT students

* Beginner developers/designers
